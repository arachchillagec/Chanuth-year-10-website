All time best Badminton player in the world

The greatest badminton player of all time is widely considered to be Lin Dan (China), who won 2 Olympic golds, 5 World Championships, and all major titles. His biggest rival was Lee Chong Wei (Malaysia), known for his speed and holding the World No.1 rank for a record 349 weeks. Other legends include Taufik Hidayat (Indonesia) with his famous backhand, and top women’s players like Zhang Ning (China), Carolina Marín (Spain), and P.V. Sindhu (India
