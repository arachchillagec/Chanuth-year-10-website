Lee Chong Wei

Lee Chong Wei is one of the greatest badminton players in history. Born in Malaysia in 1982, he started playing badminton at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks with his speed, agility, and fighting spirit. He became Malaysia top player and world number one for a record 349 weeks, including 199 consecutive weeks from 2008 to 2012.
