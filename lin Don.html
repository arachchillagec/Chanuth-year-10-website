Back ground life of the Lin don

Lin Dan is a famous badminton player from China. He was born on October 14, 1983, in Longyan, Fujian Province. Lin Dan started playing badminton when he was very young, and his parents supported his talent. At the age of 13, he joined the Peoples Liberation Army Sports Team, where he trained hard to improve his skills. He became known for his strong smashes, quick moves, and determination on the court. His hard work helped him become one of the best badminton players in the world, winning many titles and medals for China.
