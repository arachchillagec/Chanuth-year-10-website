Background life of the Lee Chong Wei

Lee Chong Wei was born on 21 October 1982 in Bagan Serai, Perak, Malaysia. He grew up in a modest family, and his parents encouraged him to play sports. At first, he liked basketball, but his mother guided him to badminton because it was played indoors, safer from the hot sun. From the age of 11, he trained seriously, and his talent was quickly noticed. He joined the Bukit Jalil Sports School in Kuala Lumpur, where he developed into one of Malaysia top young players.
